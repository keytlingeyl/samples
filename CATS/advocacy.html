WHAT DOES "ADOPT, DON'T SHOP" MEAN?

The phrase "Adopt, Don't Shop" was created to raise awareness about all the animals in shelters. Our advocacy encourages people to adopt cats and dogs from shelters or rescues them rather than purchase dog or cat from a breeder or puppy mill. Adopting from a shelter has many advantages, including providing an animal with a loving home they have never had before, freeing up space in rescues so that more animals can be save and being less expensive. Consider adopting an animal rather than shopping! It will definitely be worth it.
